# Methodology

## Research Objective

Evaluate the feasibility of explainable deep learning models for AI-assisted cervical cytology screening.

---

# Research Workflow

## Step 1 — Literature Review

Review existing papers focused on:

- Cervical cytology AI
- Medical computer vision
- Explainable AI
- Deep learning in healthcare

---

## Step 2 — Dataset Exploration

Initial dataset:

- SIPaKMeD

Tasks:

- Image visualization
- Class distribution analysis
- Data quality assessment

---

## Step 3 — Data Preprocessing

Potential preprocessing steps:

- Image resizing
- Normalization
- Augmentation
- Noise reduction

---

## Step 4 — Baseline Model Training

Initial architectures:

- CNN baseline
- Transfer learning models

Evaluation metrics:

- Accuracy
- Recall
- Sensitivity
- Specificity

---

## Step 5 — Explainability

Explainability techniques under consideration:

- Grad-CAM
- Attention maps

---

# Reproducibility

All experiments should include:

- Fixed random seeds
- Versioned datasets
- Saved configurations
- Logged metrics

---

# Ethical Considerations

This project is currently intended for research purposes only.

The system is not designed or validated for clinical diagnosis.

Human clinical validation would be required for real-world deployment.
