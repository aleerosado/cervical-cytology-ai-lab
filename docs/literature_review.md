## SIPaKMeD: A New Dataset for Feature and Image Based Classification of Normal and Pathological Cervical Cells in Pap Smear Images

### Objective

Introduce a publicly available dataset for cervical cell classification.

### Dataset

SIPaKMeD dataset.

### Key Observations

- Multi-class cervical cell classification
- Dataset designed for machine learning research
- Images extracted from PAP smear slides

### Technical Notes

- Dataset size may limit generalization
- Transfer learning may be necessary
- Potential risk of overfitting

### Relevance

Primary dataset candidate for the initial research phase.
